About Me

Learn About Me Personally (Games, Dancing, Food!)
Hello, my name is Bunrith Buth. I was born and raised in Long Beach, California. My ethnicity is Cambodian (Khmer). My favorite hobbies are playing games and dancing.

For games, I enjoy playing RTS/MOBA games, such as, DOTA2 and Starcraft. As for now, I currently play DOTA2 and MapleStory2 on my free time. That's of course, when I'm not studying web development or working, *haha.

As for dancing, I do not partake in any social activities or groups. I find it more of a stress-reliever. I enjoy dancing all kinds of genres-- from break-dancing and popping to contemporary. I used to spend hours a day dancing as an adolescence, but due to priorities, I do not dance often.

I am a big foodie.. who's not.
